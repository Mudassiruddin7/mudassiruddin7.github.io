The Problem

Perception is an edge-deployed sensory intelligence platform designed to replace the 90-year-old white cane. 253 million people worldwide are visually impaired. The tools available to them have not meaningfully evolved in nearly a century.

The Solution

By fusing computer vision, spatial audio, and on-device ML, Perception gives visually impaired users real-time environmental awareness without cloud dependency, without expensive hardware, and without compromising privacy. It runs on hardware that costs less than a dinner for two.

The Constraint

Under $20 hardware. Zero internet requirement. Under 80ms latency. This is not a research prototype. This is a product constraint that forces every architectural decision to be justified by real-world usability.

The Status

In progress. Active development. Open to collaboration with researchers, NGOs, and accessibility-focused organizations who understand why this matters.
